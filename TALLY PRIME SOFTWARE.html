Accounting is the process of recording, summarizing, and analyzing financial transactions. It provides insights into a company‚Äôs financial health. Core concepts include:

  • Assets: What a business owns (cash, buildings, stock).
  • Liabilities: What a business owes (loans, creditors).
  • Income: Earnings from sales and services.
  • Expenses: Costs incurred to run the business.
  • Capital: Owner‚Äôs investment in the business.

Golden Rules of Accounting:

  • Debit what comes in, credit what goes out.
  • Debit the receiver, credit the giver.
  • Debit all expenses and losses, credit all incomes and gains.
